About

Leah Marie

Leah Marie is a cellist and percussionist with a passion for expressive performance and musical storytelling. Her work spans classical, contemporary, and collaborative performance settings.

With years of experience performing in ensembles and solo settings, Leah brings depth, sensitivity, and technical precision to every piece. Her musical style blends discipline with emotion, creating performances that resonate deeply with audiences.

When not performing, Leah enjoys teaching, composing, and exploring new musical collaborations.
