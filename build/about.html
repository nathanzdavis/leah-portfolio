About

Leah Mosier

Leah Mosier is a cellist and percussionist who loves using music to tell meaningful stories. She performs across a wide range of styles, including classical and contemporary music, and enjoys working in collaborative settings.

With years of experience in both ensemble and solo performances, Leah brings care, expression, and strong technical skill to every piece she plays. Her approach to music balances precision with emotion, helping her connect with audiences in an honest and engaging way.

Outside of performing, Leah enjoys composing, exploring outdoors, traveling, writing calligraphy, cooking, and spending time with friends and family.
